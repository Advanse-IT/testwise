Implementation

Up and running
in one sprint.

No rip-and-replace. Testwise connects to your existing JIRA, CI/CD, and test infrastructure.

01 — Connect

Connect your stack

OAuth-based integration with your JIRA instance, CI/CD pipeline, and test framework. Read and write scoped, fully auditable. Typically completed in a single day with no downtime.

02 — Configure

Configure your gates

Select which stages require human approval before proceeding. Start with all gates enabled for maximum oversight. Most teams turn them off one by one over 2–3 sprints as confidence in the AI output grows.

03 — Launch

Run your first autonomous sprint

Testwise reads your sprint stories, generates test cases, writes and runs automation scripts, triages defects, updates JIRA tickets, and delivers a signed-off report. Your team ships. QA happens automatically.

04 — Evolve

Earn full autonomy

The system tracks its own defect classification accuracy against your team's decisions. When its triage matches yours 95% of the time, you'll have clear data to retire each gate. Autonomy earned through evidence, not assumption.
