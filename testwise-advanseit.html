Implementation

Up and running in one sprint.

No rip-and-replace. Testwise connects to your existing JIRA, CI/CD, and test infrastructure.

Step 01
Connect your stack
We connect Testwise to your JIRA instance, CI/CD pipeline, and test framework. OAuth-based, read/write scoped, fully auditable. Takes less than a day.
Step 02
Configure your gates
Choose which stages require human approval. Start with all gates on if you're cautious — most teams turn them off one by one as confidence grows over 2–3 sprints.
Step 03
Run your first autonomous sprint
Testwise reads your sprint stories, generates and runs tests, triages defects, updates JIRA, and delivers a sign-off report. Your team ships. QA happens automatically.
Step 04
Turn off gates as trust grows
The AI tracks its own accuracy. When its defect triage matches your team's assessment 95%+ of the time, you'll know exactly which gates to retire. Full autonomy, earned through evidence.
